Avatar: The Last Airbender

About Avatar

Avatar: The Last Airbender is an American animated television series created by Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko. It first aired on Nickelodeon from February 21, 2005, to July 19, 2008.

The series is set in a world where some people can manipulate the classical elements with psychokinetic variants of Chinese martial arts known as "bending". The show follows the adventures of Aang, the last surviving Airbender, and his friends as they try to save the world from the Fire Nation.
